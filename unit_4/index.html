About Us

With the publication of the Kothari Commission's report on education, the urgent need for Schools with a common syllabus throughout the country was felt. Although the Govt. of India and many states took steps in order to honour the spirit of Kothari Commission, till the late sixties only a few hundreds schools could be established. The rapidly burgeoning demand for such schools made the Children's Education Trust Of India, founded in 1966, realise its duty towards the need of our society. With the inspiration from the great educationalist late Dr. Sarvapalli Radha Krishnan, the Children's Education Trust of India constituted the Central Academy Educational Society, which made a long term programme to establish such institutions at various places all over the country.

Our Vision

Central Academy is dedicated to the development of wholesome foundation for a generation which would mould the destiny of our country in the 21st Century. It is an Institution with its eyes firmly focused on the future. We believe that freedom of spirit and zeal for discovery are as important as the need for discipline and a sense of duty. We aim to develop both the mind and the body of the child and strongly emphasize the need for an all round development of the pupil's personality.



Student Facilities

  • Facilites of a well equipped library with books on various subjects, encyclopaedias and periodicals are available for students from class II to XII. In the pre-primary & primary classes, picture reading & story telling form a regular feature of instruction
  • Laboratory facilities are available for Physics, Chemistry and Biology
  • The Computer Centre provides an adequate number of computers with latest features to impart computer education from class 'V' onwards.
  • Large & Spacious playgrounds are available with facilities for Football, Cricket, Badminton, etc.
  • T.V. & Video/ Other Audio Visual equipment are also available to screen educational and socially relevant programmes for children.
  • The school provides transport to the student. A fleet of buses carry the student to & from the school. Rickshaws are also provided on certain routes where bus cannot ply.
